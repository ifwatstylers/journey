Contest Rules


1. Weekly winner will be choose based on creativity and effort. Each submission must come together with #travelwithPLUSMiles. Tagging @plus_malaysia and location of where the photo was taken are mandatory.

2. Weekly winners, both grand and consolation, are required to collect as many kilometers as possible on PLUS highways to be eligible to become the monthly winner.

3. All winners must collect kilometers along the PLUS highways to stand a chance to win the monthly prizes and grand prize.

4. Grand prize winner will be chosen based on the numbers of kilometers accumulated throughout the contest period. Only monthly winners can win the grand prize; 3 grand and 6 consolations.

5. Participation will be evaluated by our panel of private referees consist of PLUS Malaysia Berhad.

6. Participation is PLUS Miles account bound and each master account can only win one weekly and monthly prizes. Should the winner have more than one PLUS Miles cards, the kilometers from all accounts registered under the master account will be accumulated.

7. Entries shall not contain or related to sensitive issues - at PLUS Malaysia Berhad's absolute discretion - being defamatory, obscene, violent or inappropriate.

8. All decisions made by the Organizer in respect of every aspect of this Contest shall be final, binding and shall not be disputed. Any discussions, correspondence, inquiries, appeals and challenges regarding the Organizer's decision will not be entertained.
